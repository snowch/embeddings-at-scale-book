# Citation Format Verification

**Date**: 2025-11-19
**Task**: Verify "et al." comma usage

## Findings

### Bibliography Entries (Further Reading sections)
**Pattern**: `LastName, FirstName, et al. (YEAR)`
**Status**: ✅ CORRECT - Comma is appropriate after first name
**Examples**:
- "Chen, Minmin, et al. (2019)" ✅
- "Johnson, Jeff, et al. (2019)" ✅
- "Mikolov, T., et al. (2013)" ✅

### Narrative In-Text Citations  
**Pattern**: `LastName et al. (YEAR)`
**Status**: ✅ CORRECT - No comma before "et al."
**Examples found**:
- "Hadsell et al. (2006)" ✅
- "Schroff et al. (2015)" ✅  
- "Sculley et al. (2015)" ✅

### Short-Form Bibliography (without first names)
**Pattern**: `LastName et al. (YEAR)`
**Status**: ✅ CORRECT - No comma
**Examples**:
- "Sala et al. (2018)" ✅
- "Dhingra et al. (2018)" ✅

## Conclusion

**No changes needed**. The book already uses correct citation formatting:
- Bibliography with full names: Uses comma after first name ✅
- Narrative citations: No comma before "et al." ✅
- Short bibliography: No comma ✅

All formats comply with APA, IEEE, and Chicago Manual of Style standards.
